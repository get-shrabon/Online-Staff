Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

What services do you offer?

We provide web development, website maintenance, virtual assistant services, social media marketing, WordPress websites, and eCommerce solutions.

What's included in subscription plans?

Plans include development work, maintenance, support, and VA hours. Pro and Enterprise plans offer additional features like priority support and advanced analytics.

How long do projects take?

Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications may require 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What technologies do you use?

We work with React.js, Node.js, PHP, WordPress, WooCommerce, and modern web technologies. We stay updated with industry best practices.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, all plans include ongoing support via email, chat, or phone. We handle updates, security, and technical assistance.

How do you ensure quality?

We follow rigorous testing, code reviews, and industry best practices. Our experienced team ensures every project meets high standards.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Absolutely! We create custom pricing and solutions for unique requirements. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
